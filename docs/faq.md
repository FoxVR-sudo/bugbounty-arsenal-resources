# FAQ

## Is registration required?

Yes.

Public demo scans are available without registration.

Accounts are only required for saving scan history and workflows.

---

## Is this a hosted scanner?

The platform provides browser-based workflows and utilities for web testing and recon tasks.

---

## Is this intended for offensive use?

No.

The platform is intended for authorized security testing and educational purposes only.

---

## Are scans stored?

Scan history is only stored for registered users.

---

## Does the platform support exports?

Yes.

Reports and findings can be exported for further analysis.
